Welcome to RollCall Pro

Your attendance system solution, designed to streamline attendance management for educational institutions.

Features

  • Easy attendance marking.
  • Face recognition capability.
  • CSV file logging for attendance records.
  • User-friendly interface.

How It Works

RollCall Pro utilizes advanced face recognition technology to mark attendance seamlessly. Here's how it works:

  1. Students are registered in the system with their images.
  2. The system uses a camera to capture live images during class.
  3. Attendance is marked automatically based on the recognized faces.
  4. Attendance records are saved in a CSV file for easy access.

Usage Tips

Here are some tips to get the most out of RollCall Pro:

  • Ensure good lighting conditions for the camera for optimal face recognition.
  • Regularly update the database with new student images.
  • Review attendance logs periodically to ensure accuracy.
  • Utilize the CSV export feature for reporting purposes.
